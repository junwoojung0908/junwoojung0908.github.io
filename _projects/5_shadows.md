Implemented classical shadow tomography to detect quantum phase transitions in the 1D Transverse-Field Ising Model (TFIM). Benchmarked shadow-estimated Rényi-2 entropy against exact diagonalization across the critical point (g/J = 1).

DTU course project: Scientific Methods for Quantum Information Science (5 ECTS), Summer 2025.
